About

Learn More About Us

We're a New Delhi-based Wedding Photography and Film-making Studio, not the biggest, but definitely extraordinary. At Shubham Rajput & Bhaskar Suryavanshi, we're all about fun, creativity, and a modern touch when it comes to Indian Weddings. We embarked on this journey in 2020 and have crafted countless unique love stories from across the globe since then. We're passionate about what we do, absolutely obsessed with storytelling, and excited to collaborate with you! By the way, we've already covered over 75 weddings and counting!

Just like wedding photography, Indian Wedding Movies have transformed from simple coverage into thematic documentary style wedding films. Our wedding movies are simple stories that tell the tale of your wedding, filmed in our inimitable fun and off-beat style. Using the very latest and best technology available, our wedding films are tailor made for your big day and perfectly complement your wedding photos.

  • Wedding Films: Indian Wedding Movies have undergone a modern transformation, becoming thematic documentaries.
  • Unique Storytelling:Our wedding movies narrate your special day in a fun and distinctive style, making your memories come alive.
  • Tech-Infused Magic:We harness the latest technology to create custom films that perfectly complement your wedding photos, preserving your Indian wedding in the best way possible.

Congratulations on your upcoming wedding - that's fantastic news! Selecting the perfect candid wedding photographer is a crucial decision. After all, once the food is savored, the decorations are taken down, it's your wedding photos and film that will preserve those cherished memories for generations to come. Whether you're seeking a Candid Wedding Photographer in Delhi or any other city, this choice carries great significance for your wedding day! We understand that the abundance of options can be overwhelming. Where do you even begin? Here's the good news: there are no strict right or wrong choices. Indian Wedding Photographers are now among the best globally. The key is to find a photographer whose style aligns with the story you want to tell on your wedding day. Every Indian Wedding Photographer is unique, so we invite you to explore our website or check out our Instagram to help you decide. To learn more about our work, browse through our wedding photography and films, get to know us, or even better, reach out and connect with us! We're here to help you make your special day unforgettable.
